01 Information We Collect

VoltageChecker.website is designed to be used without creating an account or providing any personal information. However, certain data is collected automatically when you visit the site.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Server log data — Your anonymised IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and date/time of access. This is standard practice for any website and is used solely for security and diagnostics.
  • Anonymous usage data — Aggregated, non-identifying statistics such as which countries are searched most frequently. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and helps us improve the service.
  • Cookies — Small text files placed on your device as described in detail in Section 3 below.

Information You Voluntarily Provide

If you contact us via our contact form or by email, you provide your name, email address, and message content. This information is used only to respond to your enquiry and is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

✓ No account required. You can use every feature of VoltageChecker.website — including the full 195-country database — without creating an account or providing any personal information.

02 How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following limited purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve VoltageChecker.website and its tools
  • To monitor site performance, traffic patterns, and security threats
  • To respond to messages sent through our contact form or by email
  • To display advertisements through Google AdSense (see Section 4)
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third parties. We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing communications of any kind.

03 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. VoltageChecker.website uses the following categories of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They do not store any personally identifiable information and cannot be disabled without affecting core site functionality.

Analytics Cookies

We may use anonymised analytics to understand how visitors use our site. All analytics data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users. This helps us understand which features are most valuable and improve the overall experience.

Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense places advertising cookies on your device to deliver ads that are relevant to your interests, based on your browsing behaviour. See Section 4 for full details on how this works and how to opt out.

💡 Cookie control: You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling advertising cookies will not affect the functionality of the voltage checker — you will simply see non-personalised ads.

04 Advertising — Google AdSense

VoltageChecker.website uses Google AdSense, an advertising service provided by Google LLC. Advertising revenue is what allows us to keep this tool completely free for everyone, with no subscription, no registration, and no paywalls.

Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

What Google May Collect

  • Your IP address (used to infer general location)
  • Browser type, version, and device identifiers
  • Cookie data associated with your Google account (if you are logged in)
  • Browsing behaviour across websites that participate in the Google Display Network

Your Advertising Choices

You have several options to control personalised advertising:

  • Visit Google's Ads Settings to manage your preferences
  • Opt out via aboutads.info (Digital Advertising Alliance)
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Adjust your cookie preferences through your browser settings

If you opt out of personalised advertising, you will still see advertisements on our site — they simply will not be tailored to your interests. For more information, see How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.
