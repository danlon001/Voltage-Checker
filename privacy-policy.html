01

Overview

Voltage Checker ("we," "us," or "the site") is a free reference tool for travelers. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use the site.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit voltagechecker.website, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree with any part of it, please discontinue use of the site.
02

Data We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal data. There are no registration forms, user accounts, or databases on this site.

However, third-party services integrated into the site (detailed below) may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Your IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URL (how you arrived at the site)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is collected and processed by Google through AdSense and Analytics, not by Voltage Checker directly.

03

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. This site itself does not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party services embedded on this site do use cookies to function.

Types of cookies used by third parties on this site:

  • Advertising cookies — set by Google AdSense to serve personalized or contextual ads
  • Analytics cookies — set by Google Analytics to measure traffic and user behavior
  • Performance cookies — used to understand how visitors interact with the site
You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how ads are displayed, but will not impact the core functionality of the voltage checker tool.
04

Google AdSense & Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense helps keep Voltage Checker free and available to everyone.

Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites across the web. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Google's privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

05

Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site — which pages are visited, how long users stay, and how they navigate. This data is aggregated and anonymous; it is not tied to any individual user's identity.

Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages viewed and session duration
  • Country/region of the visitor (approximate, based on IP)
  • Device and browser type
  • Traffic source (e.g., search engine, direct, referral)

This information is used solely to improve the site — to understand which features are most useful and ensure the tool works well across devices.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

06

Third-Party Links

This site may contain links to external websites (for example, links to adapter or converter products, travel resources, or cookie management tools). Voltage Checker is not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit via a link from this page.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal data under laws such as the GDPR (European Union) or CCPA (California). Since Voltage Checker does not directly collect personal data, most of these rights apply to the data held by Google (Analytics and AdSense) rather than by us.

For data Google holds, you can:

  • Review and manage your data at myaccount.google.com
  • Opt out of personalized ads at google.com/settings/ads
  • Request data deletion through Google's tools

For any other privacy concerns related to this site, contact us at hello@voltagechecker.website.

08

Children's Privacy

Voltage Checker is a general-purpose travel reference tool not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this site, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

09

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the site, applicable law, or third-party services. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

Continued use of the site following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
