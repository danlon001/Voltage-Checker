03 Description of the Service

VoltageChecker provides an informational reference tool for travelers and anyone seeking to understand international electrical standards. The Service includes:

  • Voltage compatibility checker — an interactive tool allowing users to compare electrical standards between their home country and a destination country, and to check device voltage ratings.
  • Country voltage database — a reference table covering 195+ countries with information on voltage, frequency (Hz), and plug types.
  • Plug type guide — a visual reference covering the major global plug and socket standards (Type A through Type N and beyond).
  • Device compatibility guide — informational guidance on common consumer devices and their typical compatibility with international power standards.
  • Travel guides and articles — editorial content offering practical advice for travelers on power adapters, voltage converters, and electrical safety abroad.

05 Electrical Safety Disclaimer

Electrical safety is a serious matter. Incorrect use of voltage converters, plug adapters, or incompatible appliances can result in equipment damage, electrical fires, serious injury, or death.

🔴
Important safety warning VoltageChecker accepts no responsibility for any electrical damage, personal injury, fire, or other harm that may result from acting — or failing to act — based on information provided by this website. Always prioritize safety and consult a qualified professional when in doubt.

Users should be aware of the following fundamental safety principles, which are provided here for informational purposes only and not as professional advice:

  • Never plug a single-voltage device into an outlet with a significantly different voltage without an appropriate converter.
  • A plug adapter alone does not convert voltage — it only changes the physical shape of the plug.
  • Always check the input label on your device before using any adapter or converter.
  • If in doubt about electrical compatibility, consult the device manufacturer or a qualified electrician.
  • Do not use damaged, frayed, or low-quality adapters and converters.

The recommendations generated by VoltageChecker's compatibility tool are based on widely accepted general standards and are intended as initial guidance only. They should always be verified against the specific device documentation and the actual conditions at your destination.
