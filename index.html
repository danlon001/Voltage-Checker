Device Reference

Dual-Voltage vs Single-Voltage

Most modern electronics are dual-voltage and work anywhere. Heating appliances are almost always single-voltage and need a converter.

Pro Tip

Reading the label: Look for "INPUT:" on your power adapter. 100–240V = works everywhere, just need a plug adapter. 110V only = North America & Japan only. 220–240V only = Europe, Asia, Australia only. Frequency (50Hz vs 60Hz) rarely matters for modern electronics.
