// COMMON QUESTIONS
BEFORE YOU WRITE — QUICK ANSWERS

Will my laptop work abroad?

Almost certainly yes. Look for 100–240V on the power brick. If it shows that, you only need a plug adapter — no converter.

Do I need a converter for Europe?

Only if your device is 120V-only (e.g. some US hair dryers). Dual-voltage devices just need a plug adapter.

What adapter do I need for the UK?

A Type G adapter. The UK uses a unique three-rectangular-pin plug not found elsewhere in Europe.

What plug does Australia use?

Australia uses Type I — two or three flat pins arranged in a V-shape, at 230V / 50Hz.

Is VoltageChecker free?
