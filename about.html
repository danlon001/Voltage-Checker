About

Built by a traveler,
for travelers.

Voltage Checker started as a personal frustration — arriving abroad with the wrong adapter. Now it's a free resource used by travelers worldwide.

The Story

Why this exists

I've always traveled with too many gadgets and not enough knowledge about power. After frying a hair dryer in a hotel in Europe and scrambling to find the right adapter at the airport at midnight, I realized there was no fast, simple tool that just answered the question: will my device work here?

Most resources were buried in travel forums or overly complicated. So I built Voltage Checker — a no-nonsense, free reference that tells you exactly what you need before you pack your bag.

There's no account, no subscription, no paywall. Just answers.

Principles

How I try to keep this useful

Accuracy over speed. Every country entry is verified against official and technical sources. If something looks off, I'd rather update it than leave travelers with bad info.

No dark patterns. No pop-ups, no newsletter signups, no tracking walls. The site is kept alive by unobtrusive ads, which I appreciate you understanding.

Always free. Travel information should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget. That's not changing.
